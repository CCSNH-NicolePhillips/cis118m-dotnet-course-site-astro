# Week 5 Homework: Pattern Printer

## Overview
Use loops to print a simple pattern (stars, numbers, or a sequence). You choose the pattern—just follow the requirements.

## Requirements checklist (acceptance criteria)
- [ ] Uses at least one loop
- [ ] Prints at least 5 lines of output
- [ ] Output follows a clear pattern (increasing, decreasing, or repeated structure)
- [ ] Includes labels or a short header so the output is readable
- [ ] Runs without errors
