## Tips

**Stuck?** Here's a hint:

```csharp
Console.WriteLine("Welcome to CIS 118M!");
Console.WriteLine("My name is Nikki.");
```

**Common mistakes to avoid:**
- Forgetting quotes around your text
- Forgetting semicolons at the end of statements
- Misspelling `Console.WriteLine` (capital C, capital W, capital L)
- Forgetting the parentheses `()`
